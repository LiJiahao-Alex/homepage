I am an Assistant Professor at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ICT, CAS). I obtained my B.S. degree in Computational Mathematics from Shandong University (SDU) and my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from ICT, CAS, under the supervision of Yiqiang Chen. My research interests lie in:

Application: Anomaly Detection, Open-World AI, Robust Perception, Federated Learning
Direction: Memory Mechanisms, Unlearnable Data, Vector Quantization, Multi-agent collaboration, Representation Learning
Vision: Build adaptive and trustworthy AI systems beyond closed-world assumptions

I am always open to collaboration and welcome students who are interested in machine learning, computer vision, representation learning, and related areas in CS/ML/DL/AI.
